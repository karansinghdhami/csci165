Baked Sweet Potatoes

You will need:
  • 5oz of noodles
  • 3 orange
  • 5 mangoes
  • 10oz of raisins
  • 2 sweet potatoes, sliced
Instructions:
  1. Soak the noodles
  2. Dice the oranges
  3. Mash the mangoes
  4. Separate the raisins
  6. Blend the sweet potatoes
  7. Bake the sweet potatoes
  8. Squeeze the sweet potatoes
  9. Deseed the raisins
  10. Deseed the noodles>
  11. Cover the sweet potatoes
  12. Slice the noodles
